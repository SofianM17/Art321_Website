Radio Program - 'Chickenman'

Description

This adaptation of "The Origin of Chickenman" features Jamie Blatz as Chickenman / Benton Harbor, Sofian Mustafa as the Commissioner, Mary Adebayo as Miss Helfinger, and Derek Dawkins as the Narrator.


FL Studio was used to edit, mix, and render the audio. Recordings were done separately and compiled together. The background music was custom made using the same software, and various free-to-use sound effects were added to enhance the believability and feel of the radio play. Editing was done by Sofian Mustafa.
